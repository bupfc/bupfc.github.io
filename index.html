About Us


Founded in April 5, 2019, BUP Film Club is the official film society of Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP). Driven by passion for storytelling and cinematic excellence, we aim to inspire, educate, and create a community of filmmakers and film enthusiasts. From organizing the largest student-run film festival in BUP to collaborating with global organizations, we strive to celebrate the art of cinema while pushing the boundaries of creativity.
